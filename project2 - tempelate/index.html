Project Info

Our aim is to increase awareness about environmental crises, find a creative solution to increase sustainability and inform people with our project. Our project follows an innovative textile production method that uses coffee grounds and recycled polyester as main materials. In our school and the holding there are several coffee shops that throw out the coffee grounds. Coffee has a really high consumption rate especially among students and white-collar workers. Also, plastic is a material well-known for not being able to decompose in nature. Hence, these materials can be put into good use by converting them into textile since textile is a widely used material that can have various functions such as clothing, furniture and accessories like bags. Produced textiles can be used in the production factories of Tepe Home and Bilkent Store. The cloth production process is patented; thus, this might be an opportunity to have an effective investment or collaboration that both increases sustainability and introduces this innovative approach for our country.
